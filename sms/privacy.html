Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the private home automation and notification system operated by the system owner and used exclusively by authorized household members.

Who This Applies To

This is a private, household system. No products or services are offered to the public. The only individuals who interact with this system are the system owner and their spouse. No third parties have access.

Information Collected

The system processes phone numbers solely for the purpose of routing automated notifications and commands between authorized household members and the private home automation server. No data is collected from third parties.

How Information Is Used

Phone numbers are used exclusively to send and receive SMS messages between authorized household members and the private server. This includes system alerts, status notifications, home automation commands, and automated responses.

Consent

All individuals whose phone numbers are registered with this system are household members who have expressly consented to send and receive messages through the system. No unsolicited messages are sent. Participants may opt out at any time by contacting the system owner.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. All information processed by this system remains private and is used solely for the operation of the household automation system.

Data Retention

Message logs are retained only as long as necessary for system operation and troubleshooting. No long term database of messages is maintained.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the system owner through the domain registrar contact information for casaq.net.
